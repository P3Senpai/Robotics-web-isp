About the organisation FIRST and its competitions

About FIRST

FIRST is an organisation that wants to inspire a future generation about science and technology. They are involved in creating programs that help inspire engage people from around the ages of 6 to 18 years. To learn more click the button below to go to their official website.

This Years Challenge

This year's (2018/19) FRIST Tech challenge is called Rover Ruckus. You are space explorers who have traveled far and wide in serach of minerals to mine. You have found the planet and have deployed the collecting and storage rovers to the surface to collect the minerals.

FIRST Global Challange

About the organisation FIRST Global and its competition

About FIRST Global

FIRST Global is a not-for-profit organistation that holds robotics events which bring together youths from all over the world.

This Years Challenge

In 2018, FIRST Global chose "Energy Impact" as the theme for the challenge. Teams will have to earn points by utilizing different energy sources on the playing field.

Mr. Popstefanjia

Mentor

Mr. Popstefanjia (Igor) is an IT teacher at ISP. He is originally Macedonian but was raised in the United States. He has taught at ISP for three years and taught before at Nova which is located in Skopje, Macedonia and at Leulumoega Foo in Samoa. He received a BA in political science with a minor in information technology from the University of Massachusetts, Anherst.
